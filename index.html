Interpreting Your Energy

The chart above illustrates the distribution of elemental energies—Fire, Water, Air, and Earth—in your tarot reading. Each element represents different aspects of your personality and life path:

  • Fire: Symbolizes passion, energy, and creativity. A strong Fire presence indicates a dynamic and enthusiastic approach to life.
  • Water: Represents emotions, intuition, and relationships. A dominant Water element suggests a deep emotional connection and empathy towards others.
  • Air: Denotes intellect, communication, and ideas. A significant Air influence reflects a thoughtful and analytical mindset.
  • Earth: Stands for stability, practicality, and material concerns. A robust Earth element points to a grounded and reliable nature.

Understanding the balance of these elements can provide valuable insights into your strengths, challenges, and overall life direction. Use this knowledge to align your actions with your true path and embrace the energies that resonate most with you.
